Dear Humans, Get Over Yourselves.

April 14th, 2012

Ok, I get it. We're big, we're powerful, and we're scary. We live in an environment that's foreign to you, which may as well make us monsters. Although Jaws may be a great film - I am - and I can't help but be a little awed by the portrayal - It's grossly inaccurate.

For starters, we don't seek out people. You're not that interesting, you present little-to-no-challenge as prey, and you're a terrible food source. You're not even an appetizer. Let Me put this is terms you can understand: do you enjoy eating ants? That is what you are to us. So stop kidding yourself. You ain't all that.

Secondly, we hate bathing suits. I know I might be overstepping my bounds by speaking on behalf of all sharks on this one, but come on. What are you trying to hide? and what' the deal with the bright colors? It's like everyone at the beach is a fan of Ted Nugent.
